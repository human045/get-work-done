Frequently asked questions

Is Get Work Done really free?

Yes. Every feature — tasks, Pomodoro timer, expense tracker, points system — is completely free. No premium tier, no ads.

How is this different from Notion or Todoist?

We combine task management with a Pomodoro timer, expense tracking, and a gamified points/leaderboard system. No other app does all four in one beautifully designed package for free.

Does my data sync across devices?

Yes — sign in with Google and your data syncs instantly via Firebase Firestore across all your devices.

Can I use it without signing in?

Yes — Guest mode gives you access to the core features without any login. Your data stays local until you sign in.
