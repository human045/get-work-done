FAQ

Questions about the expense tracker

Is this expense tracker really free?

Yes — 100% free. Sign in with Google, add your accounts, and start tracking. No limits on transactions or accounts.

Which currencies are supported?

Currently INR (₹), USD ($), EUR (€), and GBP (£). Each account can have its own currency.

Is my financial data secure?

Yes. Your data is stored in Firebase Firestore under your own Google account. Only you can access it via Firestore security rules.

Can I track multiple bank accounts?

Yes — add as many asset accounts as you need. Each tracks its own balance, and transfers between them are supported.

Does it work on mobile?

Fully responsive. The dashboard, account cards, and modals all adapt beautifully to any screen size.
