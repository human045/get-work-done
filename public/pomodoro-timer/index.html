How It Works

The Pomodoro Technique in 3 steps

Scientifically proven to boost focus and prevent burnout. Here's the loop.

🎯

Step 1 — Choose Your Work

Pick a project from your Works list and link it to the timer. Your pending tasks appear right in the sidebar.

⏱

Step 2 — Focus for 25 Minutes

Hit Start and commit to single-tasking. The progress bar fills as time passes. Tick off tasks as you complete them.

☕

Step 3 — Rest and Repeat

After 25 minutes the timer switches to Break. Rest for 5 minutes, then jump back in. After 4 rounds, take a longer break.

FAQ

Common questions

What is the Pomodoro Technique?

A time-management method by Francesco Cirillo: work for 25 minutes, take a 5-minute break, repeat. After 4 rounds, take a 15–30 minute long break.

Can I change the 25-minute default?

Yes — tap the settings icon (⚙) inside the timer to set any focus duration from 1–90 minutes and any break from 1–30 minutes.

Do I need to create an account?

No — Guest mode works without login. Sign in with Google to sync your tasks and earn points on the leaderboard.

Does it work without internet?

The timer itself works offline. Task syncing and points require a connection to Firebase Firestore.
